Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Set in the kingdom of Denmark, Prince Hamlet wants revenge on his Uncle Claudius for murdering the old King Hamlet and for also succeeding the throne and marrying Gertrude, his mother and the widow of King Hamlet. Hamlet seems to have gone mad in preparations for his revenge, and even gives away his plans to Claudius who in turn vows to end Hamlet's life. The tragedy follows Hamlet and Claudius routes to exact revenge ultimately resulting in the deaths of the whole royal family and the kingdom falling apart.
